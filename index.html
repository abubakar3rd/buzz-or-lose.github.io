How To Play

Game Instructions Buzz or Lose is a small charade game that is used to test your general knowledge skills . Each teams need to answer a question (choice of category), and perform the action exercise. The team with the highest cumulative score at the end of the game wins!

2 Teams 3players each
Ages 10 and up

Object: Score the most points after 6 rounds to win the game.

SetUp: Choose three players to keep throughout the game, A captain to represent the team.

    Playing the Game:

  • Each question in “Buzz or Lose” is of multiple-choice and it consist of 6 rounds
  • The teams can decide the number of rounds they want to play.
  • You must always answer the question correctly to score 10 points and The total score for the game is based on your responses to all questions.
  • Only the captain can play for each team so each team should choose a team captain
  • After responding to a question, click on the "Next Question" button at the bottom to go to the next question. the Game will be played respectively, when one team respond to a question, the other question will be given to the other team. If you wish to play the game again , the questions and their respective responses will be randomized.
